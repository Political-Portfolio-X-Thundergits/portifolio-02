| Legislative Impact

A series of impactful legislative contributions made to uplift education, safety, and rural infrastructure in the region.

🛡️

Women's Safety Bill

Championed a comprehensive bill ensuring women’s safety in public transport, schools, and night shelters.

👷‍♂️

Youth Employment Reform

Successfully passed amendments mandating 35% local youth employment in infrastructure and public works contracts.

⚡

Rural Electrification Push

Played a key role in policy acceleration, bringing 24/7 electricity and mobile tower clearance to over 150 remote villages.

Education Funding Act
